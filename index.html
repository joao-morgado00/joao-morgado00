Goblox A/B Test

Conducted an A/B test analysis for Goblox's e-commerce platform, focusing on a new banner's influence on customer behaviour. Utilized SQL for data organization, Sheets for hypothesis testing and confidence interval calculations, followed by Tableau for insightful visualizations. Identified an increase in conversion rates without significant spending growth. Recommended further testing and continuous evaluation, highlighting expertise in data analytics and e-commerce marketing strategies.
